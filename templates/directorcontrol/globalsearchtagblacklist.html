Global Searchtag Blacklist Information

Search tags added here will not be able to be added to the list of search tags for an item, except by the owner of the item in question. Wildcards are permitted. Acceptable formats of tags are as follows:

  • fur* - Blocks fury, furry, furred, etc.

  • *ing - Blocks running, jumping, playing, etc.
  • Normal tags may also be used: red_fox, hermaphrodite, etc.
