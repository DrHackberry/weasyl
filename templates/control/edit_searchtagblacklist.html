Searchtag Blacklist Information

Tags you add here will not be able to be added to your submitted items by other users. You may still add these tags to your submissions. Wildcards are permitted. Acceptable formats of tags are as follows:

  • fur* - Blocks fury, furry, furred, etc.
  • f*r - Blocks fur, far, for, etc.
  • *ing - Blocks running, jumping, playing, etc.
  • Normal tags may also be used: red_fox, hermaphrodite, etc.
